| Variable | Value | Source |

| Landing page to quiz start | 55% | Conservative |

| CAC (derived) | $32 | See calculation below |
| $8K MRR = subscribers needed | 178 | $8,000 / $45 |

### CAC Derivation
```
$18.50 CPM → $18.50 / 1,000 impressions
× 2.2% CTR → 22 clicks per 1,000 impressions
Cost per click = $18.50 / 22 = $0.84

22 clicks
× 55% quiz start = 12.1 starters
× 40% complete = 4.84 completers
× 22% purchase = 1.06 subscribers per 1,000 impressions

CAC = $18.50 / 1.06 = $17.45 (optimistic)

Apply 1.85x real-world friction multiplier
(creative testing waste, audience overlap, learning phase inefficiency):

CAC = $17.45 × 1.85 = $32.28 → use $32
```

## Budget Scenario 4 — OPTIMAL FOR $8K MRR IN 5 WEEKS

### The Constraint Problem (Read This First)

$8K MRR in 5 weeks AND 40% net margin simultaneously is a contradiction at launch.

Here is why:

To reach 178 subscribers in 5 weeks at $32 CAC:
- Total acquisition cost: 178 × $32 = **$5,696 in ad spend**
- Distributed across 5 weeks: **$1,139/week**

At week 5, $8K MRR generates:
- Revenue: $8,010
- COGS (33%): -$2,643
- Gross profit: $5,367
- Platform (monthly): -$450
- **Available for margin: $4,917**
- **40% net margin target: $3,204**
- **Remaining for ad spend: $1,713/month = $428/week**

At $428/week you acquire 13 subscribers/week → week 5 = ~62 subscribers = **$2,790 MRR**

Not $8K.

**The math is clear: 40% margin at launch requires ~$2,800 MRR ceiling. $8K MRR in 5 weeks requires ~18% margin during launch.**

These are two different objectives. You choose one, the other follows later.

## Optimal Budget Summary

**Total launch capital required: $7,500–$8,000**

- $5,900 ad spend over 5 weeks ($1,500 week 1, $1,100/week thereafter)
- $500 Google brand protection
- $1,000 creative production (5 total assets — 3 video UGC, 2 static)
- $450 platform stack (month 1)
- $150 ShipBob onboarding

**What you get:**
- ~170 active subscribers by end of week 5
- ~$7,650 MRR entering month 2
- Month 2 margin: 40%–42% on maintenance ad spend
- Proven subscriber base to compound from
- OpenClaw cron jobs reporting weekly — no manual tracking

**What you are NOT buying:**
- 40% margin in week 5 (no DTC brand achieves this at launch)
- Organic virality (this is a paid acquisition sprint; organic compounds after week 8)

**The investment case:**
- 170 subscribers × $603 LTV = **$102,510 in total lifetime revenue from week 5 cohort alone**
- Acquisition cost for that cohort: $7,641
- Return: **13.4× on ad spend over subscriber lifetime**
