## Identity 1 — KINETIC

**Positioning:** Performance-first. Built for people who move. The multivitamin that matches your output.

### Visual System
| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Background | Near-black `#0D0D0D` |
| Primary accent | Electric green `#00FF88` |
| Secondary | Pure white `#FFFFFF` |
| Tertiary | Dark charcoal `#1A1A1A` |
| Typography — display | Bebas Neue or Neue Haas Grotesk Display (condensed, bold) |
| Typography — body | Inter 400/500, tight tracking |
| Visual motif | Motion blur, light leaks, kinetic product photography |
| Imagery style | Athletes mid-motion, dark studio, green accent lighting |

### Brand Voice
- Direct. No fluff.
- Second person always: "Your formula. Your output."
- Never say "wellness" — say "performance," "output," "fuel"

### Tagline Options
1. **"Built for your motion."** (primary recommendation)
2. "Your formula. Your output."
3. "Move more. Recover faster."

### Meta Ad Creative Blueprint
| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Format | 4:5 vertical video, 15–30 sec |
| Hook (first 3 sec) | Black screen, green text flash: "Your multivitamin isn't built for how you move." |
| Body | UGC athlete — 3 capsules, pre-workout ritual, fast cut to workout |
| CTA overlay | "Build my formula →" |
| Burn-in subtitles | Yes — muted autoplay |
| Predicted CTR | 1.8–2.2% |

### Quiz Entry CTA
> "Build my formula"

### Best Channels
- Meta (men 25–38, fitness/gym interest targeting)
- YouTube pre-roll (gym, training content adjacency)
- Google (performance supplement intent keywords)

### Virality Pattern
High performance-angle resonance. Dark aesthetic stands out in a sea of white/pastel supplement ads. UGC in gym context triggers identity-matching behavior ("that's me").

## Identity 2 — RITUAL

**Positioning:** The personalized morning ritual. Wellness built around your life, your body, your routine.

### Visual System
| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Background | Warm cream `#F5EFE0` |
| Primary accent | Terracotta `#C4714A` |
| Secondary | Sage green `#8FA67A` |
| Tertiary | Warm off-white `#FAF7F2` |
| Typography — display | Canela or Freight Display (serif, variable weight) |
| Typography — body | Freight Sans or Lato 300/400, generous tracking |
| Visual motif | Morning light, linen texture, flat lay still life |
| Imagery style | Supplement bottle + coffee + journal on linen surface, golden hour window light |

### Brand Voice
- Warm. Intentional. Knowledgeable but not clinical.
- "You deserve a vitamin that knows you."
- Ritual language: "practice," "morning," "daily," "your body"

### Tagline Options
1. **"Your morning. Personalized."** (primary recommendation)
2. "Made for you. Every day."
3. "The ritual that knows your body."

### Meta Ad Creative Blueprint
| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Format | 4:5 vertical video or carousel |
| Hook (first 3 sec) | Flat lay pour of capsules into palm, soft morning light, voiceover: "I switched to a vitamin that actually knows my body." |
| Body | 3-step morning ritual sequence → quiz result reveal → before/after energy |
| CTA overlay | "Find my ritual →" |
| Burn-in subtitles | Yes |
| Predicted CTR | 1.6–2.0% |

### Quiz Entry CTA
> "Find my ritual"

### Best Channels
- Meta (women 28–45, wellness/yoga/self-care interest targeting)
- Instagram Reels organic
- Pinterest (high organic ceiling for this aesthetic)

### Virality Pattern
Morning routine content has proven longevity on Instagram and Pinterest. Terracotta/cream palette is Pinterest's highest-saved aesthetic in health category. Low ad fatigue vs dark/neon styles. Best for female 28–45 demo.

## Identity 3 — CLARITY

**Positioning:** Science-first, ingredient-transparent, precision-personalized. The supplement for people who actually read the label.

### Visual System
| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Background | Bright white `#FFFFFF` |
| Primary accent | Cobalt blue `#0047AB` |
| Secondary | Cool silver-gray `#8C929A` |
| Tertiary | Light ice blue `#EEF3FA` |
| Typography — display | Neue Montreal or DM Sans (geometric, clean, precise) |
| Typography — body | Inter 400, generous line height, data-table friendly |
| Visual motif | Split panels, ingredient breakdowns, precision UI elements |
| Imagery style | Clean white product, clinical flat lay, ingredient close-up, chart overlays |

### Brand Voice
- Precise. Evidence-forward. Earns trust through specificity.
- "We analyzed 14 biomarkers. Here's what your formula contains."
- Never vague. Always specific dosages, specific outcomes.

### Tagline Options
1. **"Formulated for you. Precisely."** (primary recommendation)
2. "The science behind your formula."
3. "Personalized. Verified. Yours."

### Meta Ad Creative Blueprint
| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Format | 4:5 static or slow-motion video |
| Hook (first 3 sec) | Split panel: "Generic multivitamin" (crowded pill pile) vs "Your Motion Multi's formula" (clean, labeled capsules) |
| Body | Ingredient transparency breakdown — "You got Vitamin D3 because you work indoors. You got Magnesium because you reported poor sleep." |
| CTA overlay | "See my analysis →" |
| Burn-in subtitles | Yes |
| Predicted CTR | 1.4–1.8% |

### Quiz Entry CTA
> "See my analysis"

### Best Channels
- Google Search (supplement ingredient intent keywords — high buyer intent)
- Meta (35–55, health-conscious, research-oriented)
- Amazon A+ content (highest Amazon conversion of the four identities)
- YouTube (long-form ingredient deep-dive content)

### Virality Pattern
Slower viral ceiling than PULSE or KINETIC, but highest LTV and lowest return rate. Skeptical buyers who go deep on ingredients become the most loyal subscribers. Strong Amazon and Google performance — lower Meta CTR offset by higher conversion rate and lower churn.

## Identity 4 — PULSE

**Positioning:** Vibrant, social-native, transformation-forward. You feel the difference. So does your feed.

### Visual System
| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Background | Hot coral `#FF4D4D` |
| Primary accent | Electric lime `#C6F135` |
| Secondary | Pure white `#FFFFFF` |
| Tertiary | Deep coral `#CC2E2E` |
| Typography — display | Figtree ExtraBold or Nunito Black (rounded, expressive) |
| Typography — body | Figtree 500, playful spacing |
| Visual motif | Bold split screens, before/after energy comparisons, UGC selfie aesthetic |
| Imagery style | Phone-shot UGC, bright colors, genuine facial expressions (energy, surprise, joy) |

### Brand Voice
- Energetic. Community-first. Relatable, not aspirational.
- "Day 1 vs Day 14. I'm not the same person."
- Emojis used sparingly in ads. Heavy in SMS/Postscript flows.
- First-person testimonial voice dominates.

### Tagline Options
1. **"Feel it working."** (primary recommendation)
2. "Day 14 energy is different."
3. "Made for you. Felt by everyone."

### Meta Ad Creative Blueprint
| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Format | 4:5 vertical UGC video, 8–20 sec |
| Hook (first 3 sec) | Selfie camera, person holds bottle: "I tried this for 14 days and I can't go back." |
| Body | Fast cuts: morning energy, workout, afternoon focus — no crash |
| CTA overlay | "Get my formula →" |
| Burn-in subtitles | Yes — large, bold, lime green |
| Predicted CTR | 2.0–2.7% (highest ceiling of all four) |

### Quiz Entry CTA
> "Get my formula"

### Best Channels
- TikTok (primary — algorithm rewards this exact format)
- Meta Reels (same creative, repurposed)
- Instagram Stories
- UGC seeding with micro-influencers (10K–100K followers)

### Virality Pattern
Highest viral ceiling of all four identities. TikTok algorithm surface area is wide for transformation content. "Day X vs Day Y" format has proven repeat virality in supplement category. Most shareable. Highest CTR but potentially higher churn if audience is less committed to supplement routines — offset by strong Klaviyo/Postscript retention sequences.

## Recommendation

**Lead with PULSE for launch.** Highest CTR ceiling, lowest creative production cost (UGC-native), and fastest algorithm learning phase on Meta. Pair with RITUAL as the A/B variant in the same ad set — identical quiz funnel, different creative skin. CLARITY becomes the Amazon listing identity when that channel opens. KINETIC is the Phase 2 creative refresh at week 6.

**Week 1–2:** PULSE creative (3 UGC variants) → quiz funnel → RITUAL creative (2 static variants) → same quiz funnel
**Week 3:** Read CTR + CVR data → double down on winner
**Week 6:** KINETIC creative batch as refresh when primary identity shows fatigue (ads burn at 4–6 week cycle)
