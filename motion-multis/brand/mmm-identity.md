Not the ingredients. Not the health outcome. Not the personalization engine.

Coffee. Chew. Mmm.

Three seconds of sound that sell themselves. The ad is the experience.

## Sensory Identity

The brand lives in three sounds in sequence. Every ad, every piece of content, every Postscript SMS opens with this or references it.

```
[coffee sip]  →  [lip smack: "ahhh"]  →  [chew]  →  "Mmm."
```

This is the brand's sonic logo. It is always the hook. It is never explained.

### Why this works
- ASMR-adjacent content is the most rewatched category on TikTok in health/wellness
- Sound-driven ads cost less to produce than visual-driven ads (one person, one phone, one morning)
- The sequence triggers genuine sensory anticipation — viewers feel it before they think about it
- "Mmm" is the most universal expression of satisfied eating across every language and culture
- Chewable supplement content is a nearly empty shelf on TikTok — zero saturated competition

### Color Palette
| Role | Color | Hex | Reference |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary background | Warm espresso | `#2C1A0E` | Dark roast, depth, morning |
| Primary accent | Sunrise gold | `#F0A500` | First light, warmth, energy |
| Secondary | Cream | `#FAF3E0` | Steam, softness, comfort |
| Tertiary | Caramel | `#C68642` | Mid-tone bridge, tablet color |
| Text on dark | Warm white | `#FFF8F0` | Never pure white — always warm |

### Typography
| Role | Typeface | Weight | Feel |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display / wordmark | Canela or Freight Display | Light to Medium | Warm, editorial, morning paper |
| Body | Lato or DM Sans | 300–400 | Clean, readable, never clinical |
| Brand tag "Mmm" | Custom or Canela Italic | Medium Italic | The single most important glyph in the system |

### Visual Motif
- Morning surface: dark wood, warm ceramic mug, cream linen
- Steam rising from coffee cup adjacent to product
- Close-up of chewable tablet — texture visible, not a pill
- Hands cradling a warm mug, not reaching for a pill bottle
- Window light — never studio flash. Always soft. Always warm.

### Product Photography Direction
Shot 1: Overhead flat lay — espresso cup (half-full), MMM tablet tin, morning light from left
Shot 2: Side profile — steam rising, tablet in palm, soft-focus background
Shot 3: Close-up macro — tablet texture, the bite, the "Mmm" moment
Shot 4: Lifestyle — person at window, coffee in one hand, tablet in other, eyes closed, slight smile

## Voice & Copy

### Brand voice
Sensory. Minimal. Never explains itself. Lets the feeling do the work.

### Principles
- Never say "multivitamin" in the first 5 seconds of any ad
- Never lead with an ingredient claim — lead with the sensation
- Always write like someone who just woke up and had a great first moment of the day
- Short sentences. Or fragments. Like this.

### Tagline (audio-first — written form is secondary)
> *[coffee sip, lip smack "ahhh"], [chew sound] "Mmm."*

**Written rendition:**
> "Mmm. That's your morning."

**Alt taglines:**
- "Good morning starts here."
- "The vitamin you actually look forward to."
- "Morning, made better."

## Product Supplier Flag

**CRITICAL — Phase 2 dependency:**
MMM is a chewable product, not a capsule. Supliful's primary catalog is capsules, softgels, and powders. Before finalizing this brand direction:

1. Confirm Supliful offers a chewable tablet format in their white-label catalog
2. If not: evaluate alternative white-label chewable supplement manufacturers
   - Option A: Makers Nutrition (chewable white-label, FDA-registered facility)
   - Option B: NutraceutixRx (chewable + gummy white-label, MOQ review required)
   - Option C: Custom contract manufacturer (higher MOQ, higher margin, 90-day lead time)
3. Chewable format must support AI-personalized ingredient variation — confirm manufacturer can produce multiple SKU variants at viable MOQ

**This supplier check is the first task in Phase 2 execution.**
